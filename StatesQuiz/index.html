States Quiz Support

We think States Quiz is the coolest, funnest, way to learn the States and their Capitals. If you need any help, please see the questions below. Still need help? Email us at support@downrightsimple.com
  • How do I play?
    Launch the app and tap the big "Play Quiz!" button.

  • How do I choose a category?
    After you tap "Play Quiz!" from the main screen, tap one of the 3 categories to begin!

  • How do I answer a question?
    Tap one of the 4 answer buttons at the bottom of the screen. Be careful to answer correctly, or you won't score any points!

  • I got a question wrong, how do I continue?
    If you get a question wrong, States Quiz shows you the correct answer. Tap the button to move to the next question.

  • I finished the game, can I play again?
    Yes you can! Just tap the "Play Again" button to return to the main screen.

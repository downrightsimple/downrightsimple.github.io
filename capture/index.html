Capture Support

We think Capture is the easiest, fastest note taking app on the App Store. If you need any help, please see the questions below. Still need help? Email us at support@downrightsimple.com
  • How do I save?
    Capture saves automatically when you make changes.

  • Can I save multiple ideas at once?
    Yes! Type your first idea, tap "Save and New", and then type your next idea! You can Capture as many ideas as you want!

  • I quit the app, where did my idea go?
    Capture automatically saved your idea and is now ready to Capture your next idea. You can view all your existing ideas by tapping the "View List" button. Your newest ideas will be at the top.

  • How do I see all the ideas I captured?
    Tap the "View List" button from the main screen.

  • How do I delete an idea?
    From the "View List" screen, tap the "Edit" button. Find the item you want to delete. Tap the red circle next to the item. You can then tap "Delete" to confirm that you want to delete the item.

  • How do I organize my list?
    From the "View List" screen, tap the "Edit" button. Find the item you want to move. Tap and hold the three-lines on the right of the item, and just drag it to where you want it in the list and let go.

  • Can I edit an existing idea?
    From the "View List" screen, tap the item that you want to edit. From the review screen, just tap the text and start editing,

  • How can I share an idea?
    From the "View List" screen, tap the item that you want to share. From the review screen, tap the Share button in the upper right. You can then email the idea, send it as a message, etc.

  • How do I get an idea out of Capture?
    From the "View List" screen, tap the item that you want to share. From the review screen, tap the Share button in the upper right. You can then copy the idea, email it to yourself, etc.
